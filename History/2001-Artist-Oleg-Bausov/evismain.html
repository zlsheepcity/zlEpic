 OLEG BAUSOV
Original painting
Wall-paintings
Drawing

Was born in Riga (Latvia) in 1949. Russian.
He has graduated from Latvian Academy of Arts in painting.

1979 Design and of the Assemble hall of the "autoelektropribor" -factory (Riga,Latvia)
1981 Monumental wall - painting and design of the Omsk's milkfabrik (Sibiria? Russia)
1982-1983 Design of the "Zaporozhstal" - metallurgic factory (Zaporozhje, Ukraine)
1984 Wall - painting of hall of Childfoodfabric (Balta, Ukraine)
1986 Design of Kirov's regional Vasnetchovy Museum of Fine Art (Viatka, Russia)
1981-1991 Design and monumental wall - paintings of the Radiofabric (Vinnica,Ukraine)
1988 Latvian Underground exhibition (Riga,Latvia)
1989 Exhibition "Young painters from Latvia" Konrad-Adenauer-Shtiftung (Bonn, Germany)
1990 Exhibition Gallery "Jaskin" (Amsterdam,Holland)
1990 Exhibition "Kunst-kaffe" (Aahen, Germany)
1991 Exhibition, performance "Action Я" (Jurmala, Latyia)
1991 Exhibition "Kulturcentrs" (Duren, Germany)
1992 Exhibition "Good-bye, papa" the Palace of Culture of the "VEF" -factory (Riga, Latvia)
1993 Painting of ceiling in private house (Ragana, Latvia)
1993 "Auction L" (Berlin, Germany)
1994 Exhibition in Bank "Lettika" (Riga, Latvia)
1998 Personal exhibition in Gallery "Krasts" (Riga, Latvia)
1998 Wall - painting of the Church of Peter and Paul (Yaffa, Israel)
1998 Personal exhibition in the Gallery "Nellia" (Riga, Latvia)
1999 Personal exhibition in the Gallery "Nellia" (Riga, Latvia)
2000 Exhibition of Fine Art (Germany)
Pictures are in the Museum of Art Duren (Germany); Vasnetchovy regional Art Museum (Viatka, Russia), private collection in Russia, Byelorussia; Germany; France; USA; Holland.
