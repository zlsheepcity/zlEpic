Murray out of Monte Carlo with back problem

April 17, 2007
Sport

The world number 10 has withdrawn from the Monte Carlo Masters after suffering a back injury in doubles on Monday

Andy Murray has withdrawn from the Monte Carlo Masters after suffering a back injury on Monday.The world number 10 injured himself while playing in a doubles match with his brother Jamie against Mariusz Fyrstenberg and Marcin Matkowski of Poland.

"I hit a shot and my back went into spasms and got worse and worse after every game," Murray said.
